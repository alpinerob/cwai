How CWAI Works
Three steps. Your master's lifetime of knowledge — preserved.
01

Capture

Hit record on your iPhone. Narrate while you work or debrief after. The AI listens for procedures, parts, specs, warnings, and timing — everything that makes you the expert.

02

Organize

AI transcribes the recording, extracts each step in sequence, identifies parts with exact specifications, and flags safety-critical warnings. Raw voice becomes a structured Job Profile.

03

Replay

Any team member opens the profile and follows it step-by-step — with the master's original voice attached to each phase. The map replaces memorization. The knowledge stays.
